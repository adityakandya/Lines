Rules:

1. You have to prevent any line to intersect with any other line.
2. Alse you have to prevent your money(shown in top left part of the screen) from going to 0.
3. Left Clicking on any point on the screen causes it to make a line to that position.
4. You can use Right Click on a line to erase that line.
5. Making a line and deleting a line uses 1 unit of your money and on the other hand collecting a coin gives you 2 units.
